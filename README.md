🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Walmart store sales data to uncover **hidden trends, seasonal patterns, and the impact of macroeconomic factors** on retail performance.

Instead of just analyzing numbers, this project answers:
> **Why sales change and how businesses can respond**

---

🎯 Business Problem
Retail businesses often struggle to understand:
- Why sales fluctuate across time  
- How external factors impact revenue  
- When to optimize inventory and pricing  

This project solves these problems using **data-driven insights**.

---

📊 Dataset Description
The dataset contains:
- 🏬 Store  
- 📅 Date  
- 💰 Weekly Sales  
- 🎉 Holiday Flag  
- 🌡 Temperature  
- ⛽ Fuel Price  
- 📈 Customer Price Index (CPI)  
- 👨‍💼 Unemployment Rate  

---

🔍 Analysis Breakdown

1. Yearly Sales Analysis
- Identified overall business growth trends  
- Highlighted best and worst performing years  

2. Quarterly Insights
- Maximum sales across quarters  
- CPI trends indicating inflation impact  
- Seasonal demand patterns  

3. Correlation Analysis
- Fuel Price vs CPI  
- Sales vs Temperature  
- Sales vs Unemployment  

4. Statistical Analysis
- Fuel price volatility (Standard Deviation)  
- Sales variability insights  

---

📈 Key Insights (Business Focused)

✔ Sales show **strong seasonal spikes**, especially during peak quarters  
✔ Rising fuel prices contribute to **increased inflation (CPI)**  
✔ Higher unemployment leads to **reduced consumer spending**  
✔ Temperature influences **shopping behavior and footfall patterns**
